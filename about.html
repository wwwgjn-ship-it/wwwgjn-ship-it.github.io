About Us

Founded in Beijing, China, BEIJING ZI YUE HAI SHI CULTURAL MEDIA TECHNOLOGY CO., LTD is an international film rights and licensing company dedicated to bringing high-quality Chinese films to audiences around the world.

In addition to representing premium Chinese titles, we actively acquire outstanding films from global markets and distribute them across China. Our expertise covers a wide range of genres including action, thriller, sci-fi, animation, and other high-value commercial content.

We provide comprehensive services such as international distribution, sales representation, festival promotion, and worldwide licensing. With an extensive network and deep understanding of both domestic and overseas markets, we strive to connect exceptional films with viewers everywhere.
