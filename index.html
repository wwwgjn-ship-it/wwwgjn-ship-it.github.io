About Us

The company, founded in Beijing, China, is an international film rights and licensing enterprise committed to bringing high-quality Chinese films to the global market.

At the same time, we acquire outstanding titles from around the world and distribute them in China.

We specialize in action, thriller, sci-fi, animation, and premium genre films, offering comprehensive services including international distribution, sales representation, and worldwide licensing.
