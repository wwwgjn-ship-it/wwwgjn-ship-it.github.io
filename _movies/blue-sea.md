In a quiet seaside village lives Xiaokui, a lively girl with many dreams. She longs to play with her friends and help her mother Hongju perform Maoqiang Opera. After Xiaokui’s father went missing at sea, Hongju shoulders the burden of supporting the family while continuing to shine on stage.

When Xiaokui falls ill and the opera loses its audience, their lives are thrown into turmoil. Despite its moments of hardship, *Blue Sea* is a poetic and heartwarming film about resilience and the strength to rise above loss.
