In ancient times, the Jiao Demon King attempted to overthrow the Three Realms but was sealed inside the Shenji Tower. A Jiao Dragon Pearl escaped and later reappeared in the mortal realm. The God of Thunder foresees that if the pearl reunites with the Demon King, disaster will follow. Lei Zhenzi and Nezha are sent to retrieve it, while Jiang Ziya recommends Erlang Shen to capture Mo Qilin, the divine steed.

Erlang Shen unexpectedly encounters a young Jiao Dragon searching for the Bao Lian Lantern. Their fates intertwine as the Jiao Demon King begins to awaken, threatening both mortals and immortals. Only by finding the Demon King’s seven weak points can the world be saved.
