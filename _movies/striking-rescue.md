Retired security expert Bai An kidnaps a wealthy young girl, He Ting, intending to use her as leverage for revenge against her father. Instead, he ends up saving her from a massacre carried out by drug traffickers.

Despite her young age, He Ting is unexpectedly mature. With her father’s fate uncertain, she proposes an unlikely alliance with Bai An — the very man who once sought revenge against her family. Together, they embark on a dangerous journey in search of the truth.
