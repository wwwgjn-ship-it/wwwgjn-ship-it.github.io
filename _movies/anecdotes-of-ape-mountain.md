Legends tell of Ape Mountain, once home to a mysterious Ape Kingdom. At its heart lived a rare white ape, worshipped as a deity known as the Ape God. Recently, rumors spread claiming the Ape God has become furious and will no longer protect the kingdom, while the king falls gravely ill.

To uncover the truth and calm the people, Master Yanyi of Taiji Village sends his disciple Xuancheng to investigate. What he discovers beneath the ancient legends will change the fate of the kingdom…
