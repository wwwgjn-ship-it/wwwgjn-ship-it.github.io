*Nominated for Best Feature Film — 75th Berlin International Film Festival, Generation Kplus.*

Inspired by Qiang mythology, the film follows a monkey raised by humans who travels to the sacred mountain in search of the mystery of “warmth,” following the footsteps of his mother, Awubaji. He seizes the fire from the “Beast of Fear,” is engulfed in flames, sheds his fur, and is reborn as a human.
