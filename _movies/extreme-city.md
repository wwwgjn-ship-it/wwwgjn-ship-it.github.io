Li Mingyang, an ordinary office worker, is transported into a desolate world known as *Extreme City* after scanning a QR code. This lawless realm is eerie, violent, and governed only by survival — achieved through defeating randomly appearing Enforcers.

Pushed to the limits of humanity, Li Mingyang faces brutal tests of friendship, love, and courage. As he and his companions seek escape, the true mastermind behind Extreme City begins to reveal itself.
