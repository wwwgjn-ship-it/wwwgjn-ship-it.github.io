Ten years ago, officer Yuan Wenshan was sent to a small town to investigate a major child abduction case. Before he could make progress, a shocking crime occurred: singer Guan Xiuying was found strangled in a car, with all valuables missing, suggesting a robbery gone wrong.

Under intense pressure, Yuan Wenshan has only seven days to uncover the truth behind the murder, the secrets hidden within the town, and the ghosts of the past.
