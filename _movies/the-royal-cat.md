“The Royal Cat” tells the story of young Bao Zheng, who arrives in Jiangning Prefecture and encounters the bizarre “Human-Animal Metamorphosis” case. During the investigation, Bao Zheng meets a stray cat with a severed tail named Zhan Ao, and the two reluctantly join forces to uncover the truth.

As the mysterious “tanuki for crown prince” conspiracy unfolds, Zhan Ao becomes a wanted criminal, Bao Zheng is imprisoned, and a deeper plot surrounding the Mid-Autumn Festival banquet begins to emerge. Meanwhile, Zhan Ao’s hidden past slowly comes to light…
