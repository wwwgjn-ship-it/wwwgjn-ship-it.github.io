Uncle Hua, a respected elder living on an old street, runs a small antique shop. When the area is forcibly demolished by the Qinglong gang, a shocking discovery is made inside the wall of his shop: six bodies, seven passports, and a cache of firearms.

At the same time, a homeless man named Old Cao witnesses everything, while a mysterious organization begins hunting Uncle Hua. Who is he really? Layers of truth and deception begin to unravel…
