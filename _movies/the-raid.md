Captain Chen Fei of the Municipal Narcotics Division follows a challenging trail of evidence in her latest case. When the undercover lead suddenly goes dark and the investigation reaches a standstill, Chen relies on her sharp instincts and relentless determination to trace the clues in reverse, gradually uncovering a deeply concealed drug-manufacturing hideout.

In the end, working in perfect coordination with an undercover officer who has long infiltrated the criminal organization, she conducts a precisely planned operation that successfully brings down the entire drug-trafficking ring led by the figure known as “Ding Gong.”
